About बै-Thak

बै-Thak is an open mic platform in Vapi, Gujarat where performers express freely – comedy, poetry, rap, singing, storytelling! We’re the FIRST in Vapi to allow non-veg & dark humor creatively.

बै-Thak is not just an open mic platform — it’s a celebration of raw expression, unfiltered talent, and unapologetic creativity. It’s a space where voices of all kinds come together — whether loud, soft, rhythmic, rebellious, or poetic — to be heard, felt, and celebrated. Whether you're a seasoned performer or someone taking the mic for the first time, बै-Thak is the stage for stand-up comedy, poetry, storytelling, singing, rapping, or anything else that flows from your soul. Got a quirky talent or a genre-defying act? Bring it on — we’re all ears.
